About DINO

Dino is a user-friendly tool designed to help you create and generate routes for outdoor activities such as running, walking, and biking. Whether you're planning a casual stroll or a thrilling bike ride, Dino simplifies the process by providing an intuitive interface to generate and customize your routes with ease. Its main goal is to offer a seamless experience, making outdoor planning effortless and fun for everyone.

Services

Route Creation with Interactive Map
Dino allows you to easily create personalized routes using an interactive map. Simply select your starting point and customize your route as you go, making it perfect for running, walking, or biking. The map lets you adjust the waypoints with ease, ensuring your route is exactly how you envision it.

Route Generator
For those who love spontaneity, Dino offers a random route generator. This feature generates waypoints based on your preferences, allowing you to quickly create a route from scratch. Once the waypoints are generated, you can fine-tune them to better suit your desired path, giving you full control over your adventure.
